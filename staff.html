Our Dedicated Staff

At Milestone School, we believe that a great school is built on the strength of its people.
Our dedicated team of educators and support staff work together to create a learning environment
where every child feels valued, challenged, and inspired to reach their full potential.

Each member of our staff embodies the school’s motto “Humble Grooming for a Bright Future”
by fostering excellence, discipline, and integrity in all that they do.
